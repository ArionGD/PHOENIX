### 🛡️ Expansion Blueprint: The Ultimate Phoenix Terminal

The Phoenix Terminal is already operating at an elite level with your **Cash Flow Orchestrator**, **AVS Predation Engine**, and **Compound Growth Oracles**. However, to upgrade it into a true **Institutional-Grade Sovereign Union Terminal**, here are the exact foundational engines you should deploy next:

#### 1. The Passive Income & Yield Protocol (Dividends)
Right now, we project wealth purely on capital appreciation (CAGR) and SIPs. We need a module to track actual **liquidity regeneration**.
*   **Feature**: A dividend/yield tracker that logs any passive income (from Stocks, Bonds, FDs, or Crypto Staking).
*   **The Visualization**: A futuristic calendar UI showing upcoming dividend payouts by month, predicting exactly when cash will spontaneously arrive in your registry without you lifting a finger.

#### 2. Tax Liability & Loss-Harvesting Engine
No high-level wealth strategy is complete without minimizing government leakage.
*   **Feature**: A terminal page dedicated to Short-Term (STCG) and Long-Term Capital Gains (LTCG) tracking.
*   **The Alpha**: An automated **"Harvesting Radar"** that highlights losing positions in your portfolio that you should sell *today* to offset the taxes on your massive winning strikes, legally protecting your capital.

#### 3. Macro Sentiment & Fear Index (Live NLP)
Since the **Altair** backend identifies vulnerable "Glass Pillar" companies using news sentiment, we should bring a macro version of that to the dashboard.
*   **Feature**: A **Market Fear/Greed** gauge integrated into the sidebar or top header.
*   **The Alpha**: It scrapes top financial headlines every hour and boils them down to a single "Market Heat" score, letting you know instantly if the market is bleeding (buy signal) or euphoric (sell signal).

#### 4. The FIRE Countdown Oracle (Financial Independence)
We have the CAGR Oracles, but we need to translate that into a defined escape velocity.
*   **Feature**: A dynamic countdown clock that merges your **Cash Flow Orchestrator** (Expenses) with your **Compound Engine** (Wealth).
*   **The Calculation**: It calculates your exact "Cost of Living" and divides it by your "Expected Yield" to project the exact Year, Month, and Day you hit **Financial Independence / Retire Early**. 

#### 5. Leverage & Obligation Scrubber (Debt Management)
While we track expenses, we aren't tracking the math of debt destruction.
*   **Feature**: A debt tracking matrix for home loans, car loans, or active margins.
*   **The Alpha**: A "Pre-payment Strike Simulator." It shows you mathematically how putting an extra ₹10,000 against a 9% loan literally guarantees a risk-free 9% return, visually mapping how many years of interest payments you destroy in seconds.
